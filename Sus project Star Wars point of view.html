Star Wars From a Cretain Point of view

From a Certain Point of View is an anthology of 40 short stories that was
released on October 3, 2017, in celebration of the 40th anniversary of Star
Wars. The first book in the From a Certain Point of View series, each short story
is told from the perspective of background characters in Star Wars: Episode IV
A New Hope.

From a Certain Point of View: The Empire Strikes Back is a book published
by Del Rey. It features forty stories set during the period of Star Wars: Episode
V The Empire Strikes Back to celebrate the film's 40th anniversary. The forty
stories' main characters were revealed on October 6, 2020.

From a Certain Point of View: Return of the Jedi is a canon anthology of
forty short stories by forty different authors in celebration of the 40th
anniversary of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. The book wa
published by Random House Worlds on August 29, 2023.
